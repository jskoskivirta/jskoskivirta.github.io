My research interests include Shimura varieties, particularly their stratifications in positive characteristic, automorphic forms, and arithmetic applications within the framework of the Langlands program.

I completed my PhD at the University of Strasbourg under the supervision of Henri Carayol and Christophe Cornut. I previously held the position of Associate Professor at Saitama University in Japan.
